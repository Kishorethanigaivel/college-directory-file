About Our College

Established in 2001, Karpaga Vinayaga College of Engineering and Technology is one of the premier institutes in Tamil Nadu, providing quality education and fostering a community of intellectual curiosity and innovation. Our campus offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses, including:

  • Bachelor of Engineering (CSE, ECE, EEE, Civil, Mechanical)
  • Master of Engineering
  • Bachelor of Technology
  • MBA and more...
